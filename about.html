Sulong, para sa Pilipino

Sulong is an end-to-end solution geared towards public utility vehicles that aims to generate passive income for PUV operators and cooperatives, lower the costs and barriers of entry for advertisers, create value for investors and the general riding public by providing realtime status of PUVs to shareholders and franchise operators, as well as provide peace of mind to the riding public by providing jeepney occupancy and location status.
